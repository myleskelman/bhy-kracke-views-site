Overview

Bhy Kracke Views is a personal creator tool used by a single operator to capture and publish timelapse videos filmed from Bhy Kracke Park, Queen Anne Hill, Seattle. It is not a public-facing application and is not available to other users.

Use

This application is operated solely by its creator for personal content publishing purposes. There are no accounts, subscriptions, or services offered to the public.
